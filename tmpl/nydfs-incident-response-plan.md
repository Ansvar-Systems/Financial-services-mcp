### Regulation-Specific Requirements
- [ ] Board-approved IR plan covering all 500.16(a)(1)-(7) elements.
- [ ] Role matrix including CISO, IT, legal, communications, and executive sponsor.
- [ ] External communication playbook covering regulator, law enforcement, and customer notification.
- [ ] Annual (minimum) tabletop exercise with attendee log and after-action report.
- [ ] Post-incident revision log showing plan updates after real incidents or exercises.
