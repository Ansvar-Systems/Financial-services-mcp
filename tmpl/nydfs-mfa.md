### Regulation-Specific Requirements
- [ ] Inventory of all remote-access entry points (VPN, SSO, direct admin interfaces).
- [ ] MFA configuration evidence for each entry point with factor types.
- [ ] Privileged account MFA coverage report (100% required).
- [ ] Compensating controls and CISO approval for any MFA exceptions.
- [ ] MFA bypass audit log and review cadence evidence.
