### Regulation-Specific Requirements
- [ ] Board-approved third-party service provider security policy.
- [ ] Risk-tiered due diligence questionnaire responses per TPSP.
- [ ] Contractual security addendum covering encryption, MFA, notice of incidents, and audit rights.
- [ ] Periodic reassessment schedule (annual minimum for critical TPSPs).
- [ ] TPSP inventory showing current risk tier, last review, next review.
