### Regulation-Specific Requirements
- [ ] Identify systems where audit trails are mandated under 500.06(a)(1) vs (a)(2).
- [ ] Document log sources, log schema, and retention for each system.
- [ ] Verify audit trails are protected against tampering (WORM, immutable storage, or hash chains).
- [ ] Confirm retention meets 5-year or 3-year requirement per subparagraph.
- [ ] Test restoration of audit trails from backup within review period.

## Evidence Collection Checklist
- [ ] Policy and procedure artifacts attached and current.
- [ ] Operational logs and reports retained for the full review period.
- [ ] Exception approvals captured with approver and expiry date.
- [ ] Remediation tickets linked to findings with closure evidence.
- [ ] Independent review evidence captured (if required).
