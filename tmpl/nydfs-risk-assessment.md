### Regulation-Specific Requirements
- [ ] Document risk assessment methodology and scoring criteria.
- [ ] Identify threats, vulnerabilities, and business impacts for each information system in scope.
- [ ] Evaluate controls against inherent risk and document residual risk.
- [ ] Re-run the risk assessment before any material technology or business change.
- [ ] Capture CISO sign-off and board-level briefing evidence.

## Evidence Collection Checklist
- [ ] Policy and procedure artifacts attached and current.
- [ ] Operational logs and reports retained for the full review period.
- [ ] Exception approvals captured with approver and expiry date.
- [ ] Remediation tickets linked to findings with closure evidence.
- [ ] Independent review evidence captured (if required).
